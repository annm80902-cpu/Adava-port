The Director

Building brands by hand

Ameh David is the director and creative lead behind Adava Arts — a design studio built on a simple belief: small businesses deserve visuals that feel considered, not templated.

Currently balancing secondary school with a growing client list, David works across two disciplines: hyperrealistic graphite and charcoal portraiture, and graphic design for promotional media — flyers, menus, and social campaigns for local food and lifestyle brands.

What started as a personal sketchbook practice has grown into a studio serving real clients with real deadlines, with every project treated as a chance to sharpen the craft.

Collaborations & team work: Adex Graphics · Leun-Emmy Graphics
